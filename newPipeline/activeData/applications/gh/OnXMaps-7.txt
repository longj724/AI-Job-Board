    <div class="content-intro"><h3>ABOUT onX</h3>
<p>As a pioneer in digital outdoor navigation with a suite of apps, onX was founded in Montana, which in turn has inspired our mission to awaken the adventurer inside everyone. With more than 300 employees located around the country working in largely remote / hybrid roles, we have created regional “Basecamps” to help remote employees find connection and inspiration with other onXers. We bring our outdoor passion to work every day, coupling it with industry-leading technology to craft dynamic outdoor experiences.</p>
<p>Through multiple years of growth, we haven’t lost our entrepreneurial ethos at onX. We offer a fast-paced, growing, tech-forward environment where ownership, accountability, and passion for winning as a team are essential. We value diversity and believe it leads to different perspectives and inspires both new adventures and new growth. As a team, we’re hungry to improve, value innovation, and believe great ideas come from any direction.</p>

    <h3>WHAT YOU WILL DO</h3>
<p>onX is looking for a <strong>Senior Director of Growth, Product</strong> to spearhead growth for our products, including onX Hunt, onX Offroad, and onX Backcountry. In this role, you will lead the Growth and Personalization team and collaborate with multiple teams like Growth Marketing and Marketing Automation to develop and implement a unified growth strategy. Your aim is to enhance customer interaction with our products by creating a customer-centric experience. As a leader, you will guide and influence a cross-functional team, mentoring team leaders to achieve ambitious growth objectives. This position reports to the Head of Product.</p>
<h3>As an onX Senior Director of Growth, Product your essential job duties and responsibilities will look like:&nbsp;</h3>
<ul>
<li>Put the customer at the center of our growth strategy, driving more users to interact with our products, connecting them to the value in our products, and ensuring our methods of communication are purpose-built for our customers</li>
<li>Partner across cross-functional key areas across the growth lifecycle (including new user acquisition, activation, subscription conversion and retention) to define and evangelize an end-to-end company-level growth-generating vision and strategy&nbsp;</li>
<li>Connect cross-functional teams, both those with and without direct supervision responsibilities, to dissolve the silos between teams, build a culture of collaboration that views growth as a collective endeavor to win at, and leverage multiple teams to get to the best results.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Lead the Growth and Personalization cross-functional product domain team (consisting of product management, product design and engineering team members)&nbsp;<br>
<ul>
<li>Mentor and coach leaders on the team, helping them develop and manage their teams</li>
<li>Guide the team to drive attributable impact, setting ambitious and measurable goals that align to the broader growth strategy</li>
<li>Define key activation moments, ensure monitoring and measurement capabilities are in place, track and leverage to drive results</li>
<li>Develop a culture of rapid experimentation and data driven decision making that leads to results while preventing duplication of efforts and cross-experiment invalidation/breakage</li>
<li>Identify and pursue new opportunities in our products that can lead to step change growth performance</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a suite of system capabilities that support the growth lifecycle, from subscriptions and payments to personalization and marketing technologies, making transitions as needed to support different stages of growth and maturity</li>
<li>Help growth-related teams through stages of the product development life cycle, including ideation, prioritization, implementation, analysis, and reporting</li>
<li>Work closely and develop strong relationships with cross functional peers including business unit leaders and functional leaders</li>
<li>Weigh several, and often conflicting, constraints and make rapid decisions</li>
</ul>
<h3>WHAT YOU’LL BRING</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:</strong>
<ul>
<li>You have a bachelor's degree or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>You have at least 10 years of experience in roles focused on driving growth for SAAS digital products, specifically with mobile-first strategies.</li>
<li>You have leadership experience as a Senior Manager or Director in fast-growing, complex organizations (e.g.: multiple product offerings, 300+ employees).</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>INDUSTRY EXPERTISE:</strong>

</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>SKILLS:</strong>
<ul>

<li>You excel in building cooperative relationships across various departments.</li>

<li>You have a solid technical understanding, connecting technology with business needs effectively.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>OTHER:</strong>

<strong>How do you know if you have a disability?</strong>
