    <h2>About Vercel:</h2>
<p>Vercel’s Frontend Cloud provides the developer experience and infrastructure to build, scale, and secure a faster, more personalized web. Customers like Under Armour, eBay, The Washington Post, Johnson &amp; Johnson, and Zapier use Vercel to build dynamic user experiences on the web.</p>
<p>At Vercel, our mission is to enable the world to ship the best products and that goes hand in hand with creating an environment where you can do the best work of your life.</p>
<h2>&nbsp;</h2>
<h2>About the Role:</h2>
<p>To further our commitment to providing an exceptional experience to our Enterprise customers, Vercel is looking to add another Customer Success Manager (CSM) to our fast-growing Customer Success team. The Customer Success Manager will own a portfolio of customer accounts and drive greater business value for them by bringing Vercel's best ideas, innovations, and capabilities to those customers.</p>
<p>The CSM role is pivotal in managing everything post-sales in the Enterprise customer lifecycle, including detailed customer onboarding, the setting and measuring of success criteria, issue resolution and escalation, identifying and generating expansion opportunities, and management of the customer renewal.</p>
<p>The role requires a background working with developers, with a strong emphasis on frontend development, cloud infrastructure and networking, and modern application deployment methods. The ability to coordinate well and work with many customers across multiple time zones is crucial, as is building long-standing relationships with product champions, users, and executives for your assigned accounts, working closely with them at all times to continually improve their teams' Vercel experience and ROI.</p>
<p><strong>This is a remote position which preferences candidate location to be aligned with Pacific or Mountain US time zone.</strong></p>
<h2>What You Will Do:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Manage a portfolio of Enterprise customers.</li>
<li>Be a trusted product advisor by aligning Vercel solutions with the customer's business strategy.</li>
<li>Drive incremental value by leading the customer through training and adoption of Vercel's best practices.</li>
<li>Continuously deliver Positive Business Outcomes by guiding the customer through their journey while driving adoption across the Vercel Maturity Model.</li>
<li>Enable customers on how best to use Vercel's solutions to Develop, Preview, and Ship their web properties.</li>
<li>Leverage account tiering to prioritize your account portfolio while ensuring retention and growth for you book of business.</li>
<li>Maintain high levels of customer engagement and satisfaction with a focus on customer loyalty.</li>
<li>Facilitate Executive Business Reviews with economic decision makers and Vercel executive sponsors.</li>
<li>Build and maintain account plans, including organization mapping and opportunity whitespace.</li>
<li>Act as the Voice of the Customer to align our resources and efforts to improve the customer experience.</li>
<li>Partner with the Sales team to help identify and raise expansion opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<h2>About You:</h2>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of professional experience in B2B / SaaS</li>
<li>Experience owning end-to-end post-sales experience and success for Developer Tools, Infrastructure, and/or Open Source products.</li>
<li>Experience managing Enterprise accounts and maintaining relationships with developers to C-suite level.</li>
<li>Strong collaboration mindset &amp; capabilities; Comfortable with asynchronous remote work.</li>
<li>Knowledge of CI/CD, deployment pipeline, and the SDLC process.</li>
<li>Comfortable managing accounts with varying requirements.</li>
<li>Demonstrable self-starter, curious, and solution focused.</li>
<li>Strong initiative, positive attitude, and both the ability and willingness to learn technical concepts.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Bonus If You:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Have devised and put in motion growth and activation strategies.</li>
<li>Experience and proficiency with Front-end web development libraries and frameworks like React, Node, Next.js, Express etc.</li>
<li>Can demonstrate an understanding of a CDN and/or Proxy.</li>
<li>Understand well, and can communicate clearly about DNS.</li>
<li>Have experience working with Enterprise applications at global scale.</li>
<li>Have previously worked at a high growth SaaS company.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2>Benefits:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Great compensation package and stock options.</li>
<li>Inclusive Healthcare Package.</li>
<li>Learn and Grow - we provide mentorship and send you to events that help you build your network and skills.</li>
<li>Flexible Time Off - Flexible vacation policy with a recommended 4-weeks per year, and paid holidays.</li>
<li>Remote Friendly - Work with teammates from different time zones across the globe.</li>
<li>We will provide you the gear you need to do your role, and a WFH budget for you to outfit your space as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The San Francisco, CA OTE pay range for this role is $115,000-150,000. This salary range is an estimate. Actual salary will be based on job related skills, experience and location. Pay ranges outside San Francisco may be adjusted based on employee location. The total compensation package also includes benefits and equity-based compensation. Your recruiter can share more about the specific pay range for your location during the hiring process.</p>
