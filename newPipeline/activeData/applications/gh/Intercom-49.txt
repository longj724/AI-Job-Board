    <div class="content-intro"><h2>Intercom is an AI-first customer service platform that helps businesses deliver better, faster, more personalized support.</h2>
<p>Intercom is bringing AI-first Customer Service to the world, dramatically improving experiences for customers, support agents, and managers alike.Modern, fast, and easy-to-use, Intercom‚Äôs complete AI-first Customer Service Platform enhances the customer experience, improves operational efficiency, and scales with our customers‚Äô business every step of the way.     <p>Customer service teams from more than 25,000 global organizations, including Atlassian, Amazon and Microsoft, use Intercom to send over 600 million messages per month and enable interactions with over 800 million monthly active end users. <p>We‚Äôre looking for a Senior Designer for our Reporting team. This is a business critical area of Intercom where you‚Äôll be leading design on our metrics and data visualisation reporting features.&nbsp;</p>
<p>You will do meaningful product design work at a <a href="https://twitter.com/thoughtwax/status/943178223810162688">highly admired</a> product-driven company that's driving real change in the industry.</p>
<p>Each senior product designer at Intercom works in a team with a product manager, up to 6 engineers, and some also work directly with research and analytics. Together they own a part of Intercom, ship solutions to challenging problems and get feedback directly from customers.</p>
<p>We've had designers in key leadership roles, including at the exec level, from the very beginning. This has made design a core foundation of our company's DNA. This means that you'll never have to justify the importance of design.</p>

<ul>
<li>Leading design of our Reporting platform, which entails designing new Reporting capabilities, data visualizations, and metrics.</li>

<li>Partner with product managers to define and evolve product roadmaps.</li>
<li>Work closely with engineers as you build, iterate on, and ship ideas.</li>
<li>Write about, present, and share what you‚Äôre learning, both internally and with the wider community via our blog, books, and events.</li>
