    <h3>About Us</h3>
<p>Twitch is the world's biggest live streaming service, with global communities built around gaming, entertainment, music, sports, cooking, and more. It is where thousands of communities come together for whatever, every day.</p>
<p>We're about community, inside and out. You'll find coworkers who are eager to team up, collaborate, and smash (or elegantly solve) problems together. We're on a quest to empower live communities, so if this sounds good to you, see what we're up to on <a href="https://link.twitch.tv/TwitchLinkedinJD" target="_blank">LinkedIn</a> and <a href="https://link.twitch.tv/TwitchTwitterJD" target="_blank">Twitter</a>, and discover the projects we're solving on our <a href="https://link.twitch.tv/TwitchBlogJD" target="_blank">Blog</a>. Be sure to explore our <a href="https://link.twitch.tv/TwitchInterviewingJD" target="_blank">Interviewing Guide</a> and <a href="https://link.twitch.tv/TwitchInstagramJD" target="_blank">Instagram</a> channel to learn how to ace our interview process.</p>
<h3>About the Role</h3>
<p>The Video Platform team is building the future of interactive video to serve both <a href="http://Twitch.tv" target="_blank">Twitch.tv</a> and the AWS community through our recently launched AWS offering, the Amazon Interactive Video Service (IVS). With trillions of minutes of video being delivered every year on our platform, we need a Technical Program Manager (TPM) to manage the delivery of our services.</p>
<p>As a Principal Technical Program Manager, you will ensure the teams are delivering the highest quality products and services predictably while meeting the technical needs of our community. You will guide both strategy and tactical decision-making to deliver complex cross-team programs. As well as partner with program managers to help support the strategic deliverables while working with engineering to support important technical efforts. You will report to a Director of Technical Program Management.</p>
<p>This is an opportunity to work within Twitch and AWS to help define the next generation of video platforms. You will bring deep technical, program management, and leadership expertise. You will have a desire to find and implement improvements, and a desire to improve product shipment efficiencies.</p>
<p>You can sit remote or out of San Francisco, Seattle, Irvine and New York.</p>
<h3>You Will:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams, such as engineering, product, marketing, security, and legal</li>
<li>Manage complex cross-team programs from ideation to implementation to delivery</li>
<li>Create transparency and predictability with roadmaps, planning, and partner management</li>
<li>Ensure goals are met on time and hitting our quality bar</li>
<li>Manage delivery schedules and emergent tasks while mitigating risks, managing tradeoffs, and eliminating blockers</li>
<li>Set and embody the team culture and standards consistent with Twitch and Amazon principles</li>
<li>Drive alignment with AWS and Twitch business and technical leadership</li>
<li>Report to the Director of Technical Program Management for the Video organization</li>
</ul>
<h3>You Have:</h3>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of project management or technical program management experience</li>
<li>A history of shipping quality products at scale, in the video streaming space or using distributed cloud technologies</li>
<li>Ability to align roadmaps and manage disparate tasks with varying priorities</li>
<li>Experience managing multiple executive sponsors, stakeholders, and dependencies</li>
<li>Experience working with distributed teams and facilitating virtual meetings</li>
<li>Experience finding and addressing risks with all levels of leadership</li>
<li>Experience in negotiating and resolving conflict smoothly and with or without formal authority</li>
<li>Bachelors in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) field, or equivalent practical experience</li>
</ul>
<h3>Bonus Points</h3>
<ul>
<li>Experience with distributed cloud technologies and projects (AWS)</li>
<li>Understanding of real-time video streaming at scale</li>
</ul>
<h3>Perks</h3>
<ul>
<li>Remote-friendly Hybrid Workplace (eligible states: WA, CA)</li>
<li>Medical, Dental, Vision &amp; Disability Insurance</li>

<li>Breakfast, Lunch &amp; Dinner Served Daily</li>
<li>Free Snacks &amp; Beverages</li>
</ul>
